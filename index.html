Reitit for Windows Phone 8.1 delayed

Wed, 27 Aug, 2014 - about 300 words
Over the summer I have been developing a Windows Phone 8.1 version of my Helsinki metropolitan area route planner app, Reitit. The new version is already mostly feature complete. However, the release of the app will be delayed due to problems I have encountered with the new framework: The app is too slow. Page navigation is noticeably slower than in the old version. One optimization that I employed in the WP7 version was to not use data binding and instead create and update all controls in code-behind.
